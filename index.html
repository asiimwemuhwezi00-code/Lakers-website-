About The Club

Entebbe Lakers Volleyball Club was founded in 2023 by Walusimbi Yusuf. The club has participated in the 2023/24 and 2024/25 playoff seasons, establishing itself as a competitive force in Uganda's volleyball community.

📍 Training Venue: Nkumba University

📅 Training Days: Monday - Saturday

⏰ Training Time: 4:00 PM - 6:30 PM
